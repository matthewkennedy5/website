Some things I believe:

  • No one knows what any of this is
    • We will probably never know
    • Yet we forget this 99% of the time
  • Culture is the most powerful force in the universe
    • We unconsciously copy the people around us
    • There can be huge returns to doing something different
    • We care more about prestige than happiness
    • Technology changes faster than conventional wisdom
    • Most social risks and career risks are not actually dangerous
  • Pessimism is dumb
    • Pessimists sound smart, optimists are successful
    • You get multiple tries
    • Opportunity abounds
    • Most risks have asymmetric upside
  • Complexity is bad
    • Looks smart, but slows you down
    • It’s often better to delete than to add
    • Companies tend to ship their org chart
    • Most of the current software “stack” is unnecessary
  • We don't understand how big the world is
    • 8 billion people are currently living in first person
    • 190+ countries
    • thousands of cultures
    • all a plane ride away
  • Too much delayed gratification is bad
    • Extreme delayed gratification means no gratification
    • Enjoying life requires some present focus
    • A common deathbed regret is "I wish I hadn't worked so hard"
  • Starting a business is not actually that hard
    • Most real jobs are much harder
    • You can get 80+% of the results for only 20% of the effort
    • More people should try
